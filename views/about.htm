About

A website with searchable details of Old Mancunians who died in the First World War, and details of the school's links to the war more generally.

This website is based on a project undertaken by the Manchester Grammar School History and Archives Departments in 2015, which gathered this data into a Google Site. This functioned well, but lacked meaningful search capability, added along proper database structuring (JSON) in this iteration of the site, overseen by the Computer Science Department.

How it works
